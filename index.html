About us
General information

At Dot&Needle, we believe that a tattoo is not just a drawing on the skin, but a point of contact between art and personality.

We work in signature styles, combining minimalism, detailing, and deep meaning. Each sketch is a story created together with you.

Our philosophy is accuracy, sterility, individuality.

We do not work as a "conveyor belt" — only with those who are ready for a conscious tattoo.

Why choose us?

1. Personal approach to each client

2. Professional artists with experience

3. Modern equipment and flawless hygiene

4. Cozy studio atmosphere where you want to stay

Our artists
